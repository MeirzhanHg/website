JUMEIRAH

Jumeirah is one of the most desirable residential areas along Dubai's coast. It makes an ideal experience for anyone looking for a vibrant yet peaceful lifestyle. The neighborhood has a popular commercial strip offering a number of amenities close to the residential sub-communities that appeal to tourists, expats, and locals. There is a vibrant seaside feel to this community, and it is also close to one of the world's finest hotels, Burj Al Arab.

The coastal area, with its diverse housing options, offers residents not just a vibrant lifestyle but also numerous amenities. Jumeirah 1, 2, 3, City Walk and the newly created Jumeirah Bay Island are the sub-communities that make up the residential coast. Private villas, townhouses, and even a few low-rise apartment buildings are among the available properties. houses have a great amount of yard space, which is very pleasing for pets. Jumeirah is a great place to live since there are schools, hospitals, grocery stores, salons, supermarkets, entertainment, and fitness options within walking distance. Residents also have access to numerous retail options. Residents would not have to leave the community for shopping as Mercato Shopping Mall, Sunset Mall, and Jumeirah Plaza are all close by.

The Dubai Mall, the regionâ€™s much-loved shopping and entertainment center, is just 12 minutes away. The available dining options at Jumeriah will tempt your taste buds, ranging from traditional Arab food to continental gourmet options and even to Nusret's world-famous steakhouse. Due to its location on the shore, there are plenty of beaches to enjoy. Residents can visit the urban Jumeirah Beach, which is surrounded by high-rise hotels. Aside from that The Sunset Beach, is a great spot with views of the Burj Al Arab. The famous Kite Beach, which is roughly 10 to 20 minutes away, is another amazing option.
